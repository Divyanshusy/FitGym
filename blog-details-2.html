There Are So Many Different Diets Today — Paleo, Keto, Mediterranean, Raw Vegan — But They All Focus On What To Eat. Intermittent Fasting Is Different Because It Focuses On When To Eat. That’s Also Why Intermittent Fasting, Or IF, Is Also Called A Time-Restricted Diet, With One Form Called An “Eight-Hour Diet.” The Basic Idea Behind IF Is That You Put Your Body Into A Fasted State By Not Eating Or Drinking Any Calories For An Extended Period Of Time, And Then Only Eat During A Limited Window Of Hours.
